– Vincent Williams Sr.

Starting after age 30, people can lose 3-5% of their muscle mass per decade. Without regular exercise, the muscles slowly begin to deteriorate in quality and strength through a process known as sarcopenia. A solution to this dilemma is to ensure you are building an adequate balance of strength-training in with your cardio routine.
